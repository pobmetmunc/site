METMUNC XLIX

One of the best conferences on Long Island. MetMUNC, the official Model United Nation's club of POBJFKHS, strives to educate, inspire, and prepare the future generation of political thinkers and debaters. Join the club and you'll be greeted by a friendly community passionate for learning, socializing, and compromising. Members make friends, debate fiercely in conferences, and most importantly have fun!
